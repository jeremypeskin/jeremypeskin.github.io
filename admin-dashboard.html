Unanswered Questions

Is a work visa required for Canadians visiting their Company's US office?

Our Canadian company has a US office in New York City. Are Canadian employees of our allowed to visit these offices as part of a business trip without obtaining a US work visa?

Feb 25, 2016

Green Card

Your response:

Yes. Canadians, and citizens of other countries as well, can visit their company's US offices and engage in activities which are consistent with being a business visitor without the need for a work visa. Activities which are allowed include attending meetings, visiting facilities, participating in short term trainings, giving a presentation, receiving an award, and the like. Moreover, Canadians can actually be admitted as a business visitor right at the US border without first getting a visa from a US consulate.
